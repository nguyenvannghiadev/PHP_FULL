About TechUp

In short, TechUp is an IT outsourcing and business solutions provider.

TechUp, located in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh city, provides technology consulting and smart technology solutions to small and mid–sized businesses.

We offer fully managed IT solutions, from strategic technology advice and planning to nitty–gritty technical work and tech support. With our large network of resources and partners, we can handle almost any request you may have – even if you have locations outside of Hanoi or Ho Chi Minh city.

Not sure what you need? CIO Services of Techup can help with that too – we will work with you to assess your current technology and identify investments that will build your business and boost your bottom line.

It is important to us that you achieve the results you want and need. We place a high priority on understanding your goals so that we can explain technology choices in clear, non–technical terms.
